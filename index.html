Goal

Try to juggle with 3 or more snowballs.

Controls

Pass snowballs to the left with the left click.

Throw snowballs in the air with the space key. Hold longer to throw higher.

Move your mouse to grab the ball.

Press M to toggle sound.

Snowball Juggling Olympio is a 48h entry for the 2014's LudumDare gamejam 31 by Benjamin !
